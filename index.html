Rev. Dr. Corteze B. Rawley

A woman of God for the people of God, called to boldly proclaim the Gospel.

About

Rev. Dr. Corteze B. Rawley hails from Simpson United Methodist Church and has been preaching since June 10, 2001. She pursued theological education at Wesley and Palmer Seminaries and holds degrees in Religious Education, Arts, and Theology.

Ordained in 2007 and licensed as a Chaplain in 2014, she is the Founder and CEO of Breath of Life Ministries and the author of four literary works focused on faith, discipline, and spiritual growth.

Her ministry philosophy, RAWFAITH, emphasizes responsibility, accountability, inspiration, teamwork, health, hope, and healing.
