The Calling

A woman of God for the people of God. Breath of Life Ministries presents her to boldly proclaim the Gospel to touch the lives of God’s people. She hails from Simpson United Methodist Church.

Having a desire to grow more in God, she became a learned student completing course work at Wesley and Palmer Seminaries. She is a graduate of United Christian International College and Jameson School of Theology.

Dr. Rawley earned her Bachelor of Religious Education from United Christian College, Northern District, and her Master of Arts and Doctor of Theology Degrees from International Christian University. She was ordained and licensed June 10, 2007, and served as a Chaplain at Christiana Hospital Health Care Center.
